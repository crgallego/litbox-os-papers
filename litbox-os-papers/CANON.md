# The Litbox Papers Canon

## Purpose

The Litbox Papers are the living intellectual history of Litbox OS.

They document the evolution of:

- the philosophy
- the methodology
- the product
- the company
- the founder-led business operating system category

## Core Rule

> Don't write the myth. Write the discovery.

## Numbering

Canon entries use permanent numbering.

Example:

- 000 — The Collapse Before the Architecture
- 001 — The Founder Bottleneck
- 002 — The Product Council
- 003 — CCIS Review
- 004 — The Operating System Audit

Numbers do not change.

Titles may evolve.

## Editorial Standard

Do not sanitize.

Do not sensationalize.

Do not flatten uncertainty into inevitability.

Preserve the actual discovery process.
