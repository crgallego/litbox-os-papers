# Editorial Guide

## The Style

The first version of the Litbox Papers should be modular, structured, and highly reusable.

Each section should work as:

- a social post
- a slide
- a future essay section
- a book note
- internal source material

## Tone

- direct
- reflective
- precise
- emotionally true
- systems-oriented

## Avoid

- generic thought leadership
- obvious AI commentary
- hollow inspiration
- startup mythology
- forced drama

## Good Litbox Writing

Names a real pattern.

Extracts a principle.

Connects personal, operational, and organizational insight.

## Example

Bad:
AI will transform small businesses.

Good:
Organizations don't forget because people are careless. They forget because memory was never designed into the organization.
